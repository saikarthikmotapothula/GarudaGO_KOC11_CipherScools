National Packages


Delhi is the national capital of India and rightly so as it has some of the most iconic monuments of the country. People who visit Delhi usually expand their tour into visiting the NCR as well since Noida and Gurgaon have grown at a rapid rate. It is a unique blend of tradition and modernity. It is a multicultural city which celebrates many festivals. More than 70 million travellers travel from the capital city’s Indira Gandhi International Airport to hundreds of domestic and international destinations. IGI Airport serves as an origin spot for most of the tour packages offered by IRCTC. Cheap Tours from Delhi cover pilgrim destinations that are popular among tourists from all over the world. The packages offered by IRCTC Tourism from Delhi are: 1. Panj Takht Express- This is one of the most prestigious tour packages from Delhi. It covers the holy city of Amritsar. The city has Golden Temple which is a sacred destination for all the Sikh followers. The itinerary also includes visit to Patna Sahib, and Hajur Sahib Nanded for a truly religious experience of Sikhism and the history associated with it. 2. Udaipur- Venice of the East- This 04 nights/05 days trip includes excursion to major destinations of Udaipur. You can explore City Palace, Saheliyon ki Bari, Jagdish Temple, and breathtakingly beautiful Lake Pichola. You are also taken to Chittorgarh Fort by this Indian tour packages from Delhi.
